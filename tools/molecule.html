Molecule

Generate possibilities for product-market fit by listing all potential people, problems, and solutions in your target space. Align a team around a single product snapshot.

 

Draw a triangle with bubbles at each vertex. Top bubble has the word "Person", bottom-left has the word "Problem", bottom-right has the word "Solution". Participants then generate educated guesses for each bubble. One sentence for each. Compare molecules and choose one to move forward with. Treat these as hypotheses or assumptions.
