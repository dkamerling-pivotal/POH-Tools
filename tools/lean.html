Lean Hypothesis

Create experiments that companies can go out and perform to determine whether assumptions are true.

 

Fill out the template "We believe that (hypothesis). We will do/make (test). We will know this to be true if (measurable outcome and observable outcomes)." Cost, in time and budget, should also be considered. Measurable outcomes should always be set, even if they're not going to be adhered to - it helps anchor expectations and drives towards a more concrete go/no go decision.
